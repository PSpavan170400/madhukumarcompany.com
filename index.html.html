Our Story

"Take a leap of faith," they said.

Nuvedo (Nu + Ved) comes from the etymology of new and the old.
Nuvedo is on a mission to make a meaningful impact on both people and the planet, guided by the remarkable healing properties of mushrooms. With a vision focused on delivering the highest quality, trustworthy, and transparent products, we aspire to become India's leading functional mushroom brand by 2025. We are committed thought leaders in the field, dedicated to nurturing well-being and sustainability in perfect harmony. Our journey thus far is a testament to the transformative power of mushrooms, as we pave the way for a healthier, more harmonious world for all. #InMushroomsWeTrust
